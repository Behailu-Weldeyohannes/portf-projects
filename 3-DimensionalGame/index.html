2-Dimensional JavaScript Game

How To Play

You can use your mouse or keyboard keys to move the paddle and hit the ball up and break the blocks.

If you break all the balls,you win and get a Congrats alert. If you miss the ball, your score and blocks will reset.
Good Luck!
